Privacy Policy for StockIntel Automated Email Pipeline

Effective Date: December 2025

Overview

StockIntel Automated Email Pipeline ("the App") automates the sending of stock sentiment summaries through the user's Gmail account. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App accesses, how it is used, and what is never collected or stored.

Information the App Accesses

The App uses Google's OAuth authorization to send email through the Gmail API. The following permissions may be requested:

  • Send email on behalf of the user (scope: https://www.googleapis.com/auth/gmail.send)

Information the App Does NOT Access

The App does not:

  • Read any existing emails in the user's inbox
  • Access email metadata, contacts, or labels
  • Store any personal information on external servers
  • Collect user activity data or analytics
  • Share or sell any information

Local Processing Only

All email content generated by the App is produced locally within the user’s environment. No content is transmitted to external servers other than Gmail for the purpose of sending email.

Storage of Credentials

OAuth tokens are stored locally on the user’s machine. They are never uploaded, shared, or transmitted to any third party.

Third-Party Sharing

The App does not share any data with external vendors, advertisers, or analytics services.
